Mission

We are committed to educating and developing leaders of enterprises who create value for their stakeholders and society at large. We are equally committed to developing new scholars and teachers, and to creating and disseminating pathbreaking knowledge.

Vision

Developing ideas and leaders that transform the world—from the very center of business.
